in love with the struggle


This was a self initiated typographic project created from a few words I first heard in Tupac's "Me and my Girlfriend". To me it's more than a throwaway line, it sort of defines an approach to design and to life that I think we need to have in order to succeed. Work is tough. Life is tough. You have to learn to not only roll with the punches but be able to stand up and say 'thank you sir may I have another'. You have to fall in love with the struggle in order to appreciate the lessons learned with each setback.
