2012 influencers


Part of winning the hearts and minds of voters is getting the celebrities and influencers to publicly rally around your cause. We knew a large percentage of actors, musicians and all types of creative people were supporters of the President, and this project was an attempt to engage them more officially.

The site itself was password protected and sent out selectively. It listed six easy ways to help, ranging from wearing campaign gear in public to tweeting to performing; and featured some of our biggest public supporters: Eva Longoria, Neil Patrick Harris and Vince Carter.
