One task every designer at the campaign chose to take on was to select a quote from The President and design a typographic poster around it. We then had them screen printed and sold a limited run online. The quote I chose was:

"Hope is that thing inside us that insists, despite all the evidence to the contrary, that something better awaits us if we have the courage to reach for it and to work for it and to fight for it."

I would have just called it "Hope" but Sheperd Fairey beat me to it.

On one of the President's visits, Design Director Josh Higgins surprised me by getting one of them autographed for me.
