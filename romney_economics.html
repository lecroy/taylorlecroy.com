romney economics


When the Romney camp began running ads touting Mitt's business record as a possible economic savior, we saw an opportunity to tell the truth about some of the reasons behind his success. Romneyeconomics.com told four distinct stories of Bain Capital's money-over-people philosophy, complete with in-depth documentary footage of the cities, workers and townspeople that depended on the jobs eliminated by Bain buyouts and foreclosures.
